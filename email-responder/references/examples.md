## Key Chain Reply Patterns

### Pattern: Acknowledge â†’ Action â†’ Close
Most chain replies follow this simple structure:
1. Acknowledge what was said/sent
2. State what you did or will do
3. Brief close

### Pattern: Align â†’ Schedule
When discussion needs to move to a call:
1. Affirm the point ("That makes sense")
2. Propose meeting with purpose
3. Offer flexibility on timing

### Pattern: Confirm â†’ Commit
When acknowledging instructions:
1. "Understood" or "Got it"
2. State the commitment going forward
3. Thanks

### When Chains Get Very Short
In rapid back-and-forth, replies can become one-liners:
- "Thanks for the quick response. Followed up and will keep you updated!"
- "Understood, I will make sure moving forward to [do X]."
- "That makes sense. Sent over a calendar invite."
